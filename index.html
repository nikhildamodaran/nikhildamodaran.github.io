I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Government and Public Policy, OP Jindal Global University since 2018. I completed my Ph.D. from Louisiana State University in 2018. I work on the area of open economy macroeconomics.
In my research, I apply insights from open economy macroeconomics to examine sub-national movers of business cycle fluctuations in countries. I focus on the interaction of local level fiscal and monetary policy to examine issues of optimal fiscal policy. I am also interested in government debt and its place in the financial markets.
I teach courses in macroeconomics at OP Jindal Global University and Monash University.
